2021 The Game Awards nominations and results 




    The event Game Awards 2021 hosted by Geoff Keighley
who was the creater and producer of the Game Awards
Started and was held to the audience who was invited
at the Microsoft Theater in Los Angeles on 
December 9, 2021






      Genshin Impact had a good year regarding its 
  nominations and results at The Game Awards 2021. 
  It was nominated for two awards and managed to 
  win one of them in the face of tough competition. 
  The two nominations were "Best Mobile Game" and 
  "Best Ongoing Game," with Genshin Impact only 
  winning the former.






    Genshin Impact is an role-playing game 
which is developed by MiHoYo/ Hoyoverse
which was first published in 2020. The
graphics of the game is anime-based 
with open world environment and just like 
other role-playing games, battle system
using weapon, elemental magics and 
character-switching


Genshin impact is an role-playing game
which consist of gacha mechanics to 
wish for the desired characters using
a special currency called "primogems",
A party which holds a maximum of 4 
characters, Adventure Rank which can 
unlock more quests and upgrade the world 
level.
